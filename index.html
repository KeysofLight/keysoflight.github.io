About

I'm Jakub, a concert pianist with a classical background from Czechia and Italy. After years of teaching and performing, I realized something was missing: light. Not stage lights, but divine light. Keys of Light is my mission to bring spiritual peace, deep beauty, and Christ-centered purpose into piano learning.

Our Vision

Most piano lessons are about pressure, technique and perfection. We're about peace, presence, and purpose. Learn with ease. Play with heart. Let the music become prayer.

Start your journey

New to piano? Or want to reconnect with your instrument? Start with light. Free lessons and spiritual encouragement are coming soon.
